Welcome to Tab Group Sync

Back up your Chrome tab groups to bookmarks — automatically, privately, across devices.

🛡️

100% Private. No accounts. No servers. No tracking. Your data never leaves your browser. Tab groups are saved as regular bookmarks — synced across devices through your own Google account, the same way any bookmark works.

Get started in 3 steps

1

Choose a backup location

Click the extension icon in your toolbar, open Settings (gear icon), and select a bookmark folder where your tab groups will be stored.

Tip: "Other Bookmarks" is a good default choice
2

Enable sync for your groups

Toggle the switch next to each tab group to start backing it up. Or enable Auto-sync in settings to sync all new groups automatically.

3

That's it — you're protected

Your tab groups are now continuously backed up. Close tabs, restart Chrome, even switch devices — your groups are safe in your bookmarks.
